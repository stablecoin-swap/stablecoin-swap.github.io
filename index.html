How it works

Stablecoin Swap in three steps

Trade from one stable asset to another while the app keeps the important swap details in front of you: route, token, chain, time estimate, slippage settings and cost.

1

Connect your wallet

Open the app and connect a self-custody wallet. You keep control of private keys and approve every action from your wallet.

2

3

Review and sign

Compare the live quote, route and expected network cost. If the details fit, confirm the transaction from your wallet.

FAQ

Stablecoin Swap questions

01What is Stablecoin Swap?

Stablecoin Swap is a non-custodial DeFi app for trading stable assets such as USDC and USDT from a wallet-controlled flow. It focuses on stable-to-stable swaps, clear route review and wallet-native signing.

02How does a stablecoin swap work?

You choose the stablecoin you want to sell, choose the stablecoin you want to receive, review the live quote and sign the transaction. The app shows the relevant route and costs before confirmation.

03Which assets and chains are supported?

The app is designed for supported stablecoins and compatible networks, including routes on chains such as Base, Arbitrum One and OP Mainnet. See the full list on the supported chains page.

04How long does a swap take?

Timing depends on the selected chain, current network conditions and the route used. The app presents the live estimate before you sign, so you can decide whether to proceed.

05What does Stablecoin Swap cost?

Costs depend on the route, liquidity, token approval state and network fees. The final quote and transaction cost are displayed in the app before wallet confirmation.

06Is Stablecoin Swap safe to use?

The app is non-custodial, so you keep control of the wallet and must review every approval and swap before signing. Always verify token symbols, destination chain, quote details and wallet prompts.
